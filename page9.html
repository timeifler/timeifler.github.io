DESC 2020 Winter Meeting - Tucson

We are looking forward to welcoming you to Tucson and the beautiful Sonoran Desert! 
Logistical information for the meeting can be found below. For meeting registration go here and the scientific meeting schedule is found here.  
Contact: Eduardo Rozo (erozo@email.arizona.edu), Tim Eifler (timeifler@email.arizona.edu), Elisabeth Krause (krausee@email.arizona.edu)

Venue:
• Monday, Jan 20, DESC School: Steward Observatory (Map), Main Auditorium (N210)
• Tuesday-Friday, Jan 21-25: Student Union, Great Ballroom and adjacent breakout rooms  

Lodging: Accommodations are booked individually. Some information below.

Travel to Tucson
• Phoenix Sky Harbor (PHX) https://www.skyharbor.com/ is a major international hub (80 mins drive from Tucson, shuttle and bus options available, e.g. https://www.flixbus.com/bus/tucson-az , https://groometransportation.com/tucson/ )
• Tucson airport (TUS) https://www.flytucson.com/ has direct flights to Dallas, LA, Chicago, Oakland, San Jose 

Local Transportation (in addition to uber/lyft/cab service)
• Public Transport: Tucson has a tram/bus system https://www.suntran.com/ and public bike system https://tugobikeshare.com/ and scooter system https://www.razor.com/share/ 
• Walk: The venue is central and several hotels are in walkable distance 
• Parking: Parking on campus is restricted to UA Parking garages and designated metered parking on a self-pay basis. Nearest Garage to Student Union and Steward Observatory is 2nd Street Garage Check Available Garages: https://parking.arizona.edu/parking/garage-full-app/index.php?temp=59  

Local Food options
• Student Union has a variety of quick food options for lunch
• Main Gate Square/University Street has a variety of food options for lunch and dinner
• LOC favorites for dinner: Cafe Poca Cosa, Seis, La Cocina, Downtown Kitchen,
