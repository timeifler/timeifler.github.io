Tim Eifler
Associate Professor of Astronomy

CV (pdf)

My research focusses on cosmological data analysis with different observables and datasets from ground and space-based surveys. I am most interested in combining weak lensing, galaxy clustering, and CMB based observables and am building models to include small-scale information in the analyses.

Eduardo Rozo
