Arizona Cosmology Fellows

The Arizona Cosmology Fellowship Program is a postdoctoral program with scientific focus on data analysis with cosmological surveys. Fellows design their projects in close collaboration with faculty in the cosmology group and have the opportunity to develop an independent science profile within ongoing and future cosmological endeavors (DES, DESI, LSST, WFIRST, SPHEREX).
